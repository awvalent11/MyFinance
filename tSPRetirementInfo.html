TSP & Retirement Info

What is TSP?


TSP stands for Thrifty Savings Program. It is a Federal Government-sponsored retirement savings and investment program. It offers the same type of savings and tax benefits that many private corporations offer thier employees under "401(k)" plans.


Blended Retirement System


If you are a service member of the uniformed services who began servign on or after January 1, 2018, your service automatically enrolled you in the TSP (or will) once you have served 60 days and 3% of your basic pay is deducted from your paycheck each pay period and deposited in the traditional balance of your TSP account.
